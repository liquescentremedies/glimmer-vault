## Maintenance vs Design Failure Pattern

**Type:** Insight
**Reusability:** High
**Category:** Problem Solving

### Description

All 5 previous cognitive infrastructure attempts (Dec 2024 cognitive DB, Mar 2026 fused DB, multiple Obsidian vault exports, DCOS research doc, INTENTLOG) failed at the maintenance stage, not the design stage. The solutions were sophisticated but required too much executive function to sustain.

### Follow-up Actions

When designing new systems, explicitly ask: what is the maintenance burden? Who triggers it? Can it run without human remembering?
