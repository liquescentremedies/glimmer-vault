Every neurodivergent person who has ever felt like they were failing at something that was supposed to be natural. Every autistic woman who spent years performing enjoyment she was not feeling and could not find language for why. Every ADHD person who watched desire show up like a flood in a new relationship and then recede in ways they could not explain or stop. Every late-diagnosed person who looked back at their intimate history through the new lens and had to sit with what they found there.

This guide covers the territory that no one has covered before.

The nervous system as the primary sex organ: what sensory processing differences actually mean across all eight sensory systems in intimate contexts, not as clinical abstractions but as real lived experience. The masking body: what decades of overriding your own signals does to your relationship with pleasure, and the specific mechanism through which neurodivergent people end up in intimate situations they could not identify as unwanted in the moment. Desire in full: hypersexuality as neurological reality, the ADHD libido and what happens to it as novelty decreases, autistic interest-based attraction, asexuality and demisexuality and queerness and why all of them appear at elevated rates in neurodivergent populations. The architecture of shame and exactly where it came from. Consent and the double empathy problem. Trauma rates that constitute a public health crisis. Rejection sensitive dysphoria, the vulnerability hangover, emotional dysregulation during and after sex. Practical navigation: sensory accommodations, recovery time, scheduled intimacy, disclosure. Medication and hormones including a full treatment of perimenopause in autistic and ADHD bodies. And the full neurodiversity justice framing: pleasure as a political claim, erotic identity as construction, and the argument that neurodivergent people who insist on full intimate lives are doing something the systems that shaped them did not intend.

This guide is long. It is designed to be read in full and returned to in parts.
