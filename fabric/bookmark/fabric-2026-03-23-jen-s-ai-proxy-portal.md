# Jen's AI Proxy Portal

[Original link](https://fabric.so/home?expandedFdocId=29092ef1-6912-4064-bd5b-ec5a463be077)

A notepad-style project brief outlining an AI agent portal that acts as Jen's digital proxy, absorbing her context to auto-extract tasks, summaries, and connections while Jen remains the client-facing interface. First use case focuses on helping her friend Jerry with tasks, events, and ideas, with accessibility-first (NAI) design and IRC chatbot integration.

## Preview
