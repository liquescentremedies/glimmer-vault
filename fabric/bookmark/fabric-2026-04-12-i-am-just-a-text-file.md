# I am just a text file.

## Or on "How AI is better at being me than me."

And now anyone can _think_ & _write_ like me.

I don't mean this metaphorically.

I spent two hours answering questions about how I think. What I believe. How I write. What I hate. What I'd never say. What makes me cringe. What makes me _me_.

I put it in a single `.md` file _(it's a text file)_.

I dropped it into a folder.

Now Claude _(or any AI)_ writes like me, thinks like me, _is_ me.

---

## 1 — I thought I was unique.

I really did. I thought my _voice_ was magical. Something I've been perfecting forever.

My entire life is built around curating things & share it to the world. I thought my writing style, my voice, couldn't be captured. It's a human thing. My soul.

And the moment I wrote it down, it became portable. Transferable. _Reproducible._ So I am not so special. I just never cared to put it on paper.

## 2 — How to transfer your taste to a machine.

**Taste isn't what you like, but what you reject.**

80% of the file is what I'm _not_:
- I do NOT start with _"In today's fast-paced world..."_
- I do NOT use _"utilize"_ or _"leverage"_ or _"synergy"_
- I do NOT write paragraphs longer than 3 sentences
- I do NOT hedge with _"I think"_ or _"perhaps"_ or _"it seems"_
- I do NOT end with summaries of what I just said

The _"do nots"_ are the taste.

### The Interview Process

The author interviewed himself using Claude with 100 questions across categories:
- BELIEFS & CONTRARIAN TAKES (15 questions)
- WRITING MECHANICS (20 questions)
- AESTHETIC CRIMES (15 questions)
- VOICE & PERSONALITY (15 questions)
- STRUCTURAL PREFERENCES (15 questions)
- HARD NOS (10 questions)
- RED FLAGS (10 questions)

### How to Use It

Save the file to a folder, then open Claude Cowork with that folder selected. Every prompt starts the same way:

> Read **[your_name].md** first. Then **[whatever you need]**.

## 3 — The difference from a normal AI

Before the file, prompts produced competent but flat, generic, obviously AI output. The middle of everything.

After uploading the file: Claude writes like you. Uses your voice. Applies your taste.

And you can upload to ChatGPT Projects, Gemini, Grok — anywhere. The file is the context. The file is _you_.

## 4 — Anyone is a text file. Even them.

If I can fit in a markdown file, so can anyone. Naval Ravikant? A markdown file. Alan Watts? A markdown file.

Your favorite creator whose voice you'd recognize anywhere? Patterns. Constraints. Preferences. All writable.

> _"Master yourself so AI can master you."_
